About Me

My journey into the world of computers began back in middle school when I was inspired by a friend who was the go-to person for any computer-related issues. Watching others rely on him sparked my curiosity, and soon, I found myself assembling computers, installing operating systems, and setting up programs. Wanting to carve out my own path, I turned to programming, starting with basic Python scripts in 9th grade. As my interests grew, I ventured into hosting a FiveM server for GTAV. Although I initially hired a friend to help, I soon took over the project myself, diving into Lua. This hands-on experience led me to take on various client projects, expanding my skills to web development with PHP and JavaScript. Eventually, my curiosity led me to explore new fields like network security, where I continued to broaden my expertise across more than five programming languages.
